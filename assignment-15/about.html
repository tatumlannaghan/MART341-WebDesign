About me!

Hi! My name is Tatum Lannaghan. I am a Media Arts student, currently enrolled at the University of Montana and projected to graduate in the spring of 2023. My focus is in filmmaking and it is my absolute passion. I love to work in any aspect of filmmaking; editing, directing, anything I can get my hands on. I love to learn and I love to work with people. I am dedicated to my craft and always put my best into whatever I am working on. Thank you so much for checking out my page and make sure to contact me for any questions.
