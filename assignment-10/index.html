Why BOWLING is the WORST first date choice.


author's note: i have recently been asked to go bowling as a first date so there's my inspiration for this webpage.

Where to begin...



Don't get me wrong, I do love to bowl.

It is what I did almost everyday during the summer and most of winter break too.

My friends and I love to hit the alley and enjoy throwin' balls at pins.


However, bowling with two people... trying to get to know each other?

A recipe for disaster.


Imagine asking someone a deep question about their childhood to unlock their mind and learn more about them but they can't answer because it's their turn to bowl.


And don't even get me started on standing their awkardly waiting for your ball to return back to you.

It's already awkward enough, and you know they have nothing else to do but to watch you cause there is no one else to talk too!
